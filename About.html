About me

Hi there! My name is Paul-Maikel (also known online as Gnimacz). I'm an 18 year old Dutch programmer following the course Creative Media and Game Technologies. I have loved games from a young age and wanted to make my own. So I decided to learn how to do just that!

I am someone who works hard and can work well in teams. I always aspire to make the best product I can.
I wish to share my excitement about games with the world through making my own and inspire a future generation of game developers.
