Sweet Defense

My role: Game engineer


Enjoy the cutest fast-paced action tower defense game with a linear story and the ability to choose multiple endings! You've been invited to the annual Honey Festival and as you help secure the Hive, the story unfolds to reveal something sinister and unexpected

FEATURES:

  • Tower Defense adventure & Mini-game! Help King Candy defend his Kingdom using his royal turrets: Donut Cop, Cupcake Candy thrower, and Frozen Ice cream while also progress a campaign story.

  • Singleplayer & Multiplayer friendly! You can either play solo or invite your friends to co-op and defend together.

  • 5 unique and different levels! Progress through the story by moving to the next level.

  • 6 Different boss fights! Face a different boss fight per level, and a final boss on the final stage.

  • Custom Physics & Cinematics! Check out our cool visuals.

  • Custom soundtracks! As always, listen to our original soundtracks in-game and on Spotify!

About the game

Sweet Defense is a minecraft bedrock edition marketplace map created by Humblebright Studios. I worked on this map as the sole programmer. So I did project file management, coding, a little bit of technical animation. For this project my general programming knowledge and thinking skills came in handy. Since minecraft uses JSON for all of its modifications knowing that was also very helpful. As the sole programmer I had to communicate with the artists on the team and make sure everything was communicated correctly when I needed something changed or added. The game itself consists of 5 levels, each introducing a new unique type of enemy and ending the level with a custom-written boss fight. The Map concludes by having the player pick between two sides and having one final, epic boss fight.
